Oli's Feijoa Tom Collins

Feijoa-forward, bright citrus, NZ gin backbone.

Ingredients

  • NZ gin
  • Feijoa puree or cordial
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar syrup
  • Soda water

Method

  1. Shake gin, feijoa, lemon, and syrup with ice.
  2. Strain into a tall glass over fresh ice.
  3. Top with soda and stir once.
  4. Garnish and serve.

Fruit Twister Cyclone

Retro cordial profile with a sharp chilled finish.

Ingredients

  • Retro cordial base
  • Cyclone-style ice element
  • Citrus garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Build over ice in a highball glass.
  2. Adjust cordial strength to taste.
  3. Add citrus for brightness if needed.
  4. Serve immediately while very cold.
