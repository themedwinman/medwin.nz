Fruit Twister Cyclone

Retro cordial brightness with Cyclone ice-block chill.

Tap to reveal ingredients & method

Ingredients

  • Retro cordial
  • Cyclone ice block (ice-cold)
  • NZ citrus garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Fill a glass halfway with ice (or use the Cyclone ice block).
  2. Pour in retro cordial to taste.
  3. Top with a splash of citrus juice if you like it sharper.
  4. Stir gently and serve immediately.

Oli's Feijoa Tom Collins

Feijoa-forward, with an NZ-gin backbone and a clean fizz.

Tap to reveal ingredients & method

Ingredients

  • Feijoa (fresh puree or cordial)
  • NZ gin (Tom Collins style)
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar syrup (to balance)
  • Sparkling soda or tonic

Method

  1. Shake gin, feijoa, lemon, and syrup with ice.
  2. Strain into a tall glass over fresh ice.
  3. Top with soda and stir once.
  4. Garnish with a citrus twist (optional).
