Oli's Feijoa Tom Collins

Feijoa-forward, bright citrus, NZ gin backbone.

View recipe

Ingredients

  • NZ gin
  • Feijoa puree or cordial
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar syrup
  • Soda water

Method

  1. Shake gin, feijoa, lemon, and syrup with ice.
  2. Strain into a tall glass over fresh ice.
  3. Top with soda and stir once.
  4. Garnish and serve.
