Oli’s Feijoa Tom Collins

Feijoa-forward, bright citrus, NZ gin backbone. Late-summer in a glass.

Pour
  • 45 ml NZ gin
  • 30 ml feijoa puree (or cordial)
  • 20 ml fresh lemon juice
  • 15 ml sugar syrup (1:1)
  • Soda water, to top
Method
  1. Shake gin, feijoa, lemon, and syrup hard with ice.
  2. Strain into a tall glass over fresh ice.
  3. Top with soda and stir once, gently.
  4. Garnish with a lemon wheel. Serve immediately.

Fruit Twister Cyclone

Retro cordial profile with a sharp chilled finish. A crowd-pleaser at the end of a long shift.

Pour
  • Retro cordial base
  • Cyclone-style ice element
  • Citrus garnish (optional)
Method
  1. Build over ice in a highball glass.
  2. Adjust cordial strength to taste.
  3. Add citrus for brightness if needed.
  4. Serve immediately while very cold.
